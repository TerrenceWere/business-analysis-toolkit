# Business Analysis Planning & Monitoring
*IIBA BABOK Knowledge Area 1*

## Overview
Business Analysis Planning and Monitoring knowledge area governs how business analysts determine which activities are necessary to complete the business analysis effort.

## Key Tasks & Outputs

### Plan Business Analysis Approach
- **Techniques**: Brainstorming, Interviews, Workshops
- **Outputs**: Business Analysis Approach Document
- **Templates**: [Business Analysis Approach Template](../../IIBA-Templates-Artifacts/Business-Analysis-Approach.md)

### Plan Stakeholder Engagement
- **Techniques**: Stakeholder Analysis, RACI Matrix
- **Outputs**: Stakeholder Engagement Approach
- **Templates**: [Stakeholder Analysis Matrix](../../IIBA-Templates-Artifacts/Stakeholder-Analysis-Matrix.md)

### Plan Business Analysis Governance
- **Techniques**: Decision Analysis, Risk Analysis
- **Outputs**: Governance Approach, Decision-Making Process
- **Templates**: [Requirements Management Plan](../../IIBA-Templates-Artifacts/Requirements-Management-Plan.md)

### Plan Business Analysis Information Management
- **Techniques**: Data Modeling, Metadata Management
- **Outputs**: Information Management Approach
- **Templates**: [Requirements Traceability Matrix](../../IIBA-Templates-Artifacts/Requirements-Traceability-Matrix.md)

### Identify Business Analysis Performance Improvements
- **Techniques**: Performance Metrics, Lessons Learned
- **Outputs**: Performance Improvement Recommendations

## Key Concepts
- **Planning**: Determining the optimal approach for business analysis activities
- **Monitoring**: Tracking and reporting on business analysis work
- **Adapting**: Making adjustments based on performance and feedback

## Related Techniques
- Stakeholder Analysis
- Estimation
- Risk Analysis
- Metrics and Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
