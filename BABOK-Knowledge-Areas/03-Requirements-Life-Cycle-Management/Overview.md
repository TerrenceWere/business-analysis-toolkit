# Requirements Life Cycle Management
*IIBA BABOK Knowledge Area 3*

## Overview
Requirements Life Cycle Management knowledge area describes how business analysts manage and maintain requirements and design information from inception to retirement.

## Key Tasks & Outputs

### Trace Requirements
- **Techniques**: Requirements Traceability Matrix
- **Outputs**: Traceability Relationships
- **Templates**: [Requirements Traceability Matrix](../IIBA-Templates-Artifacts/Requirements-Traceability-Matrix.md)

### Maintain Requirements
- **Techniques**: Version Control, Change Management
- **Outputs**: Updated Requirements
- **Artifacts**: Requirements Repository

### Prioritize Requirements
- **Techniques**: MoSCoW, Weighted Scoring, Voting
- **Outputs**: Prioritized Requirements List
- **Templates**: [MoSCoW Prioritization](../IIBA-Techniques/MoSCoW-Prioritization.md)

### Assess Requirements Changes
- **Techniques**: Impact Analysis, Change Control Boards
- **Outputs**: Change Recommendations
- **Artifacts**: Change Request Forms

### Approve Requirements
- **Techniques**: Review Sessions, Sign-off Processes
- **Outputs**: Approved Requirements Baseline
- **Artifacts**: Approval Records

## Key Concepts
- **Life Cycle**: The progression of requirements through states
- **Traceability**: Relationships between requirements and other artifacts
- **Change Management**: Process for handling modifications to requirements

- Traceability Matrix
