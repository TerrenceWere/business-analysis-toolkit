# Elicitation & Collaboration
*IIBA BABOK Knowledge Area 2*

## Overview
Elicitation and Collaboration knowledge area describes how business analysts work with stakeholders to identify, gather, and synthesize information.

## Key Tasks & Outputs

### Prepare for Elicitation
- **Techniques**: Workshop Planning, Interview Preparation
- **Outputs**: Elicitation Activity Plan
- **Artifacts**: Interview Guides, Workshop Agendas

### Conduct Elicitation
- **Techniques**: Interviews, Workshops, Observation, Surveys
- **Outputs**: Elicitation Results, Raw Requirements
- **Artifacts**: Meeting Notes, Workshop Outputs

### Confirm Elicitation Results
- **Techniques**: Validation Sessions, Walkthroughs
- **Outputs**: Confirmed Elicitation Results
- **Artifacts**: Validation Meeting Minutes

### Communicate Business Analysis Information
- **Techniques**: Presentations, Reports, Documentation
- **Outputs**: Business Analysis Information Package
- **Artifacts**: Requirements Documents, Status Reports

### Manage Stakeholder Collaboration
- **Techniques**: Conflict Resolution, Facilitation
- **Outputs**: Stakeholder Engagement Metrics
- **Artifacts**: Collaboration Plans

## Key Concepts
- **Elicitation**: Drawing out information from stakeholders and other sources
- **Collaboration**: Working together with stakeholders to achieve shared goals
- **Communication**: Ensuring clear understanding among all parties

## Related Techniques
- Interviews
- Workshops
- Brainstorming
- Observation
- Focus Groups
- Surveys/Questionnaires
