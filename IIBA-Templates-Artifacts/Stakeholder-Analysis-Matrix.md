# Stakeholder Analysis Matrix
*IIBA BABOK Reference: Business Analysis Planning & Monitoring*

## Stakeholder Register
| Stakeholder | Role | Influence Level | Interest Level | Current Attitude | Communication Needs |
|-------------|------|----------------|----------------|------------------|---------------------|
| Executive Sponsor | Decision Maker | High | High | Champion | Weekly executive summaries |
| Department Head | Business Owner | High | High | Supportive | Bi-weekly progress reviews |
| End Users | Primary Users | Medium | High | Neutral | Regular demos & training |
| IT Team | Technical Implementation | Medium | Medium | Supportive | Technical specifications |

## Power/Interest Grid Analysis
- **Manage Closely** (High Power, High Interest): Executive Sponsor, Department Head
- **Keep Satisfied** (High Power, Low Interest): Finance Director, Legal Counsel  
- **Keep Informed** (Low Power, High Interest): End Users, Supervisors
- **Monitor** (Low Power, Low Interest): External Vendors, Support Staff

## Engagement Approach
- **Champions**: Involve in decision-making and approvals
- **Supporters**: Regular updates and seek feedback
- **Neutral**: Educate on benefits and address concerns
- **Resisters**: Identify concerns and build trust gradually
